✅ OPTIMAL ENTRY WINDOW

DTE 4–5 is the sweet spot for weekly options sellers. Theta decay accelerates while gamma risk remains manageable. Enter Iron Condor or credit spreads at defined risk.

⚠️ AGGRESSIVE ENTRY — REDUCE SIZE

DTE 3 offers elevated theta but gamma risk is rising. If entering, reduce position size by 30–50%. Widen strikes further than normal. Set tighter stop losses.

🚫 AVOID — GAMMA RISK TOO HIGH

DTE 0–2 is dangerous for premium sellers. Gamma is explosive — a small price move causes outsized losses. Only close existing positions, do not open new ones.

👁 OBSERVE — TOO EARLY TO ENTER

DTE 6–7 is too early. Theta decay is minimal. Wait for DTE 4–5 before entering weekly positions. Use this time to monitor IV regime and identify your strikes.

🚫 SKIP — MAJOR EVENT IN WINDOW

A binary event within this expiry window creates unpredictable gap risk. Wait for the event to pass. Post-event IV crush creates better entry conditions.

📆 Event Risk Calendar

🏦 RBI Policy

HIGH IMPACT · Skip week

Repo rate decisions cause 2–4% Nifty moves. IV spikes then crushes post-announcement. Enter Iron Condors the week after.

📊 Union Budget

EXTREME IMPACT · Skip

Annual budget causes sector rotation and 3–6% index moves. IV peaks 2 weeks before. Post-budget IV crush is ideal for selling.

🗳 Election Results

EXTREME IMPACT · Avoid

General election results in May 2024 caused +4.3% gap up. Binary events are unhedgeable — wait for post-event normalisation.

🇺🇸 US Fed Meeting

MEDIUM IMPACT · Reduce size

FOMC decisions affect global risk sentiment and USD/INR. Nifty often gaps 1–2%. Reduce size by 50% on FOMC weeks.

💹 Monthly Expiry

MEDIUM IMPACT · Careful

Monthly expiry week brings large OI unwinding and institutional positioning. Strike pinning risk increases. Widen strikes.

📈 GDP / CPI Data

MEDIUM IMPACT · Monitor

Macro data releases cause 0.5–1.5% Nifty moves. Note release dates. If within expiry window, reduce size appropriately.

🏢 Large Cap Earnings

LOW·MEDIUM · Stock-specific

Nifty 50 components reporting in the week can cause sector-level moves. Check earnings calendar before entering.

🌐 Global Risk Events

VARIABLE IMPACT · Monitor

Geopolitical events, crude oil spikes, and currency crises create tail risk. Keep position size conservative in uncertain environments.

📋 Entry & Exit Rules

  • IV Rank above 40% — confirmed from Volatility Engine
  • DTE between 3 and 5 — in the optimal decay window
  • No major binary event (RBI, election, budget) within the expiry window
  • Sufficient bid-ask spread liquidity at your chosen strikes
  • Strike selection: OTM by at least 1 standard deviation (±1σ = Spot × IV × √DTE/252)
  • Define max loss before entry — Iron Condor wing width determines risk
  • Close at 50% of maximum premium received — this is the primary exit rule
  • Do not hold to full expiry — gamma risk accelerates in the final 24 hours
  • If 50% target reached before DTE 2, close and do not re-enter the same expiry
  • Bank the profit — do not roll or leg into new positions in the same week
  • Hard stop: exit at 200% of credit received (total position loss = 2× premium collected)
  • Delta breach: if net delta exceeds ±30, consider rolling the tested wing further OTM
  • If spot breaches short strike: close the threatened spread immediately, assess the remaining spread
  • Never average down on a losing Iron Condor — the risk/reward deteriorates sharply

Use this to determine how far OTM to place your short strikes.

±1σ Expected Move = Spot × IV × √(DTE / 252)
Example: Nifty 22,000 · IV 18% · DTE 4
= 22,000 × 0.18 × √(4/252) = ±499 points
→ Place short strikes beyond 21,501 / 22,499
