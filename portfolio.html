📐 Risk Limit Guidelines

  • Max 2–5% of account value per individual position
  • Max 10 concurrent positions across all underlyings
  • Max net portfolio delta: ±50 delta (for a ₹10L account)
  • Max net vega: ±₹5,000 per 1% IV change
  • Min theta target: ₹500/day (if primarily a seller)
  • Stop loss per position: 2× credit received for sellers
  • Delta breach (+50): Buy/sell futures or adjust call/put ratio
  • Vega breach: Close highest-vega position or buy protective hedge
  • Gamma too high: Close near-expiry ATM positions immediately
  • Single position loss > 3% account: Exit and review
  • Portfolio down > 7% in a week: Stop all new trades
